How Does LawSpot Work?

LawSpot is a website where members of the public, like you, can ask questions about New Zealand law and qualified lawyer volunteers will answer them for free. The awesome people at Community Law Wellington & Hutt Valley will check the answers before they're published online for you to browse.

Post your legal question online, using the snazzy green "Ask" button.
One of our awesome qualified lawyer volunteers will answer your question. Community Law Wellington & Hutt Valley supervises the answers, to make sure they are super awesome too.
Approved answers go up on our website. You'll receive a handy dandy email notification too. We'll try to get this to you within 1-2 working days, but some tricky ones might take longer.
