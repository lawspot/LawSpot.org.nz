How Does LawSpot Work?

LawSpot is a website where members of the public, like you, can ask questions about New Zealand law and qualified lawyer volunteers will answer them for free. There are some questions LawSpot doesn’t answer. Find out more here.

One of our amazing partner lawyers will check the answers before they're published online for you to browse.

Post your legal question online, using the snazzy green "Ask" button.
One of our awesome qualified lawyer volunteers will answer your question.
Approved answers go up on our website. You'll receive a handy dandy email notification too.
