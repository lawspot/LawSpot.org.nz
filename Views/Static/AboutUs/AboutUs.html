Management Team

Maya Shino CEO

Co-founder Maya is a solicitor by day, and the CEO of LawSpot by, er, later in the day. Maya works at Russell McVeagh, a top-tier corporate law firm. She is passionate about using technology to increase access to the law. Maya is very fond of, but highly allergic to, cats.

Bowen Pan Co-Founder

Co-founder Bowen is a serial technology entrepreneur (no, it’s not some kind of breakfast food). He is also the former head of Treat Me product and marketing at Trade Me. Bowen is about to embark upon a MBA at Stanford University. He likes karaoke – a little too much, some would say.

Elizabeth Chan Head of Partnerships

Lizzie is perfect for the Head of Partnerships role, as she has several years of experience working closely with student and community organisations. She gets her energy from a serious addiction to chocolate, and from her passion for empowering people to help themselves. Lizzie is a judge’s clerk at the Supreme Court and the current National President of UN Youth New Zealand.

Paul Bartrum Head of Technology

Paul is LawSpot’s web developer ninja. He works in the Development Team at Trade Me, and recently helped set up the Treat Me daily deals site and the Treat Me Now mobile app. Paul eats dessert first. And second. And third. He has a thing for jaffa cakes (don’t ask).

Cheryl Sun Head of Product

Cheryl is a web designer and developer. And a lawyer too! She is currently studying towards a Masters in Law from the University of Edinburgh. As LawSpot’s Head of Product, Cheryl is responsible for keeping the site looking super swish and easy to use. You wouldn’t pick it, but Cheryl is also a World of Warcraft enthusiast and cannot wait for Diablo 3.

James Whitaker Head of Marketing

James is the voice of LawSpot. He manages all of LawSpot’s marketing and social media. James is the current Marketing Manager at Trade Me (noticing a theme here?) Previously, he was a Marketing Manager for the New Zealand Police, heading the “Get better work stories” recruitment campaign. Outside of work, James enjoys surfing, fishing and spending time with his three daughters.

Our Patron

Hon Margaret Wilson DCNZM

LawSpot is thrilled to have Margaret Wilson as its patron. Margaret has had an extensive career in public service and is a former Attorney-General and Speaker of the New Zealand House of Representatives. She is a Professor of Law and the founding Dean of Law at the University of Waikato. Margaret believes strongly in advocating for social justice and would like to see New Zealand law made more accessible and understandable. She thoroughly enjoys reading thriller novels.
