Help the New Zealand community by sharing your legal knowledge. Put up your hand to become a LawSpot member today!

Be a part of an exciting new online movement

The world is going digital, so why shouldn’t the law? Think how far we’ve come since electronic legal databases were introduced - imagine doing research without them! We now take for granted the technology we use everyday, but imagine being a part of the next new legal innovation: online legal Q&A. Join LawSpot and be a part of the Next Big Thing.

Give back

Not everyone has a law degree. Becoming a LawSpot Member means you’re making the law that much more accessible to the general public. You’re also helping to channel demand for community legal services by freeing up community law centres to deal with more challenging and complex legal issues face-to-face with clients.

Network with other Members

By becoming a Member, you’ll join a team of like-minded LawSpot lawyers whom you otherwise may not have met. Although it’s early days, we’re looking to organise social events and provide networking opportunities for our Members.
