Help the New Zealand community by sharing your legal knowledge. Put up your hand to join LawSpot today!

Be a part of an exciting new online movement

The world is going digital, so why shouldn’t the law? We now take for granted electronic legal databases, but imagine being a part of the next new legal innovation: online legal Q&A. Join LawSpot and be a part of the Next Big Thing.

Give back

Not everyone has a law degree. Becoming a LawSpot Member means you’re making the law that much more accessible to the general public. You’re also helping to free up community law centre resources so that they can deal with more complex legal issues face-to-face with clients.

Network with other Members

It’s early days, but in future we’re looking to provide opportunities for you to socialise and network with other like-minded members of the LawSpot team.
